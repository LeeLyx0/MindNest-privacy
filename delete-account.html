Delete Your MindNest Account

If you would like to delete your account and all associated data, you can do so by following these steps:

Option 1: In-App (Recommended)

Open the MindNest app, go to Settings, and select "Delete Account".

Option 2: Request via Email

You can also request account deletion by emailing us at:

mindnestdeveloper@gmail.com

Please include your account email address in the request.

What Data is Deleted

  • Your account information (email)
  • All mood entries
  • All tasks and activity data

Data Retention

Your data will be permanently deleted within 7 days of your request.
